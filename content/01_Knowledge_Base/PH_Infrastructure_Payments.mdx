## 1. Digital Behavior & Connectivity
The Philippines is a "Mobile-First" nation with high internet engagement.

* **Internet Penetration**: ~83â€“84% of the population (97.5 million users) by 2025.
* **Time Online**: Filipinos rank 3rd globally, spending avg. **8 hours 52 minutes** daily online.
* **Device Usage**: **98.5%** of internet users access via mobile phones.

### Optimization Checklist
<CheckItem id="tech-mobile" severity="high">**Mobile Optimization**: Ensure the platform is responsive or has a native Android app.</CheckItem>
<CheckItem id="tech-lite" severity="medium">**Lightweight Assets**: Optimize for slower mobile data speeds (avg 24 Mbps) to reduce bounce rates.</CheckItem>

## 2. Mobile Hardware Landscape
Understanding the device landscape is crucial for QA and performance tuning.

* **OS Dominance**: **Android** holds >90% market share.
* **Top Brands (Dec 2025)**:
    1.  **Oppo** (~13.3%)
    2.  **Realme** (~12.5%)
    3.  **Vivo** (~11.1%)
    4.  Transsion (Tecno/Infinix) - Fast growing in budget segment.
* **Apple iPhone**: Niche market (~5â€“7% share) due to high cost.

## 3. Payment Ecosystem
Digital wallets have replaced cash as the primary method for iGaming transactions.

### Key Payment Methods
| Method | User Base | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **GCash** | **~81 Million** | The "Super App" of PH. Essential for deposits/withdrawals. |
| **Maya** | Millions | Strong competitor, often serves as a digital bank. |
| **Direct Bank** | Growing | InstaPay/PESONet used for larger transactions. |
| **Cash** | N/A Online | Cash is still used to load e-wallets at 7-Eleven/Pawnshops. |

**Strategy Note**: Filipinos prefer **instant, low-fee** transactions. Integrating GCash and Maya is mandatory for conversion.
