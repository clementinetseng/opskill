## 1. Game Type Preferences
Filipinos engage in diverse gambling forms, often driven by social interaction and low entry barriers.

| Game Type | Popularity | Cultural Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bingo** | **Very High** | Traditional pastime turned online. Loved by older demographics and women for its social aspect. |
| **Slots** | High | Fits the "sachet" spending habit. Players seek jackpots on small bets. |
| **Basketball** | High | The nation's #1 sport. NBA and PBA betting drives the sportsbook vertical. |
| **Card Games** | Niche/Growing | Local games like **Tongits** and **Pusoy** are moving to P2P online formats. |

## 2. Competitor Spotlight: BingoPlus vs. OKBet

### BingoPlus (The Social Giant)
* **Core Product**: Live-streamed Bingo & Slots.
* **Positioning**: "More than just a game." Focuses on community, entertainment, and charity.
* **Strategy**:
    * **Hybrid Model**: Connected online app with physical bingo halls.
    * **Mass Appeal**: Targets casual players, including non-tech-savvy older adults.
    * **Sponsorship**: Official Bingo Partner of the PBA.

### OKBet (The Sports Authority)
* **Core Product**: Sportsbook (NBA, PBA) & Esports.
* **Positioning**: "Game changer in Filipino sports entertainment." Patriotic support for local athletes.
* **Strategy**:
    * **Grassroots**: Title sponsor of MPBL (Maharlika Pilipinas Basketball League).
    * **Target Audience**: Young to middle-aged males (21â€“40).

## 3. Marketing & Engagement Tactics
* **Influencer Marketing**: Essential. Celebrities (e.g., Vice Ganda) and YouTubers are used to build trust and reach mass audiences.
* **"Pa-premyo" (Prizes)**: Filipinos love raffles and giveaways. Campaigns like "Win a House" are highly effective for retention.
* **Community**: Features like chat rooms in Bingo apps replicate the noise and social vibe of physical parlors.
